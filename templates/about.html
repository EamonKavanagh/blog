About me

My name is Eamon Kavanagh. I was born and raised in New York and recently graduated from the University of British Columbia with a M.Sc in Mathematics. My thesis focused on a chemotaxis model that you can read a bit more about here. I did my undergraduate degree at McMaster University where I graduated summa cum laude with a B.Sc (Hon) in Mathematics. Currently, I'm interested in seeing how far I can stretch the knowledge I've gained in my many years of education. This interest has caused me to explore topics such as machine learning, statistics and programming.

Right now I'm pursuing a job in data science. With all the recent information about the field on the web it's hard not to be interested. I like the fact that it's burgeoning industry with a lot of uncharted terrain and I'm very curious to see first hand how it develops and how far it may take us.

Apart from all of this, I rode a bicycle across the United States with a good friend in the summer of 2011. We wrote about the trip and kept a photo journal here.
